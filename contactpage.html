About Us

Kashasenashe Investments Limited specialises in durable, custom-printed plastic mats designed for mosques and a variety of events. We combine quality materials, precise printing, and local production to deliver products that meet aesthetic and practical needs.

Custom mosque mats — Durable, easy-to-clean designs suitable for daily use and gatherings.
Event mats — Flexible sizes and printing options for weddings, conferences and community events.
Local service — Based in Kampala with reliable production and distribution across Uganda.
